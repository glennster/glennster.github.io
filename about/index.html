Bio

Glenn has over 30 years experience in engineering and technology. He has a degree in Aerospace Engineering from Missouri University of Science and Technology. He has certificates in Open Systems Technology, Object Oriented Technology and Internet Application Development from Washington University - Center for the Application of IT. He is a PMI certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and is qualified to lead large and complex software projects. He has owned his own website design company and has designed and developed many websites. He has been a leader in high technology areas for 15 years. He is currently working in the Houston area with research interests in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks. He also enjoys tinkering with robotics, Virtual Reality and IoT technologies using Raspberry Pi, Arduino and BeagleBone.
