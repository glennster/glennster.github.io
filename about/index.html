Bio

Glenn has over 30 years experience in engineering and technology. He has a degree in Aerospace Engineering from Missouri University of Science and Technology. He has certificates in Open Systems Technology, Object Oriented Technology and Internet Application Development from Washington University - Center for the Application of IT. He first started programming in Basica on a TRS-80, later moved to Fortran and then to C/C++, Java and Python. He has been a leader in high technology areas for 15 years. He is currently working in the Houston area with research interests in Data Science, Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks.
